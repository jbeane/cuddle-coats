Corporate Affiliates

Our affiliate stores are businesses that have pledged not to sell furs in their stores. Each of them has also agreed to be a drop-off location for the Cuddle Coats program. Donate your furs to the Cuddle Coats program, then have a look around.

Everyday People buys clothes from you for cash. They have a very large, rotating inventory of clothing and accessories, specializing in vintage and newer, trendy styles for both men and women.

Earth Exchange is the most unique thrift store with a huge selection of recycled & new merchandise. Earth Exchange has the nicest collection of recycled items like music, books, movies, jewelry, household, furniture, clothing for the whole family & much more!

The Animal Ark Thrift & Pet Store generates approximately $60,000 per year for their animal shelter. Operated by a staff of dedicated volunteers, the store has many unique and upscale items for sale.

If you're a business and you would like to become a Cuddle Coats affiliate, please contact us and let us know the name of your business and your location(s). A Cuddle Coats representative will come out to collect any furs you currently have and provide literature on the program for your shop if you would like to advertise that you're a supporter.
